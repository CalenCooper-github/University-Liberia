UNIVERSITY MISSION


“TEACHING, RESEARCHING, AND SERVING COMMUNITY”

To prepare well qualified men and women for teaching, researching, and providing public and private services, and to provide truly educated men and women effective or well qualified citizens to assume leadership in their communities and the nation.

▪ Discipline
▪ Creditability
▪ Transparency
